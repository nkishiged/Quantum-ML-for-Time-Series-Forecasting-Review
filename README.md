# Quantum-ML-for-Time-Series-Forecasting-Review

Systematic review of **Quantum Machine Learning (QML)** techniques applied to **Time Series Forecasting (TSF)** between **2014 and 2025**, with a focus on **simulated and emulated quantum models** (QSVM, QNN, QELM, QRC, hybrid architectures).

This repository provides:

- The **LaTeX source** of the paper  
- The **PRISMA-related data files** (flow counts, studies metadata)  
- Simple **Python scripts** to summarise and visualise the screening and study characteristics  
- Additional **documentation** (PRISMA checklist, flow diagram)
